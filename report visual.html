About Tawsilla

Our mission is to transform transportation in Egypt through technology and fair practices.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Tawsilla was born out of frustration with existing ride-hailing platforms that charged high commissions to drivers, resulting in higher prices for riders.

We set out to create a platform that benefits both riders and drivers by eliminating commissions and focusing on local needs.

Today, we operate in 5 major Egyptian cities with plans to expand nationwide while maintaining our commitment to fair pricing and excellent service.
